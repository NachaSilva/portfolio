SAVE GORILLAS PROJECT

ONG | UX Research | UX UI Design | Visual Prototype

The main threats to gorillas are illegal hunting, disease, and habitat degradation, making it essential to raise awareness about this critical issue. While many people want to help, they often don't know how. This donation app not only allows users to contribute but also provides valuable information, news, and updates on gorilla conservation.

The project follows a structured process with several phases: conducting interviews, creating paper and digital wireframes, developing low- and high-fidelity prototypes, conducting usability studies, ensuring accessibility, iterating on designs, defining the information architecture, and implementing responsive design.

The Product:Design a user experience to help conserve an endangered species of gorillas.

Project Duration: March 2022 to July 2022

Goal: Design an app to help conserve an endangered species of gorilla.

My Role: UX/UI Design

Tools: Figma, Adobe

Frontend: HTML, Javascript, CSS

Future: The app aims to provide more educational resources for users to learn about endangered gorillas, while also offering detailed information about gorillas' lives and the actions taken by conservation organizations. Additionally, research will be conducted to assess the app's success in reaching its donation goals and raising awareness.
