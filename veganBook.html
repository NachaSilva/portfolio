VEGAN BOOK

UX UI Design | Front End | Social Media

Responsive social network for the vegan community, where users can share and content related to veganism. This project focused on user research to meet a real need in the most secure way protecting their data and in an intuitive way of navigation.

In addition to recipes, the platform offers a space to share lifestyle tips, ethical product recommendations, culinary experiences, news about the movement and related events.

Prototype: Vegan Book Platform

Methodology: Agile

Duration: 4 months

UX/UI Tools: Figma

Frontend: Git, GitHub, Javascript, HTML dinámico, CSS, UX Design

Conection: Firebase, Firestore

Development Collaboration: 3 Front End Developers

Test: Jest

Future:Basis for creating new functionalities such as live videos and events for the community.
