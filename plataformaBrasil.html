PLATAFORMA DE INVESTIMENTO

UX UI Design | UX Research | Front End

First Investment Platform for Belat Bank in Brazil. In this project, I collaborated with teams from both Chile and Brazil. I led the UX Design area, focusing on research, user profiles, flows, prototyping, user journey mapping, and conducted iterations and research with internal and external clients, including potential platform users.

For UI Design, we worked on the graphic line and design materials alongside a team of designers to ensure alignment with the brand’s identity. I also supported the frontend development.

Platform: BelatBasil

Methodology: Agile

Duration: 10 months

UX/UI Tools: Figma, Adobe

Frontend: Vue.js, Vuetify, Git, GitHub

Backend: PHP

Development Collaboration: Worked with external company Boostech

Graphic Design Collaboration: Worked with external company Ludus

Future: Basis for developing a single investment portal (Chile, Argentina, Uruguay, Brazil)
